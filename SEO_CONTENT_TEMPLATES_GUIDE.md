### Current vs. Recommended URL Structure

| Current | Recommended | Keyword | Benefits |
|---------|---|---|---|
| `/walkins` | `/walk-in-jobs` | Walk-in jobs | Clear keyword |
| `/internships` | `/internship-jobs` | Internship jobs | Explicit intent |
| `/jobs` | `/fresher-jobs` | Fresher jobs | Primary segment |
| New | `/walk-in-jobs-[city]` | Location-based | Geo-targeting |
| New | `/jobs-[city]` | City focus | Local search |
| New | `/jobs-1-3-years` | Experience level | Clear targeting |
| New | `/internship-with-job-offer` | Conversion focus | Higher intent |
